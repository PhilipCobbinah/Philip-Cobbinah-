RoboServe clinched victory at the 2025 ENACTUS National Competition! Our team from Regent University College of Science and Technology emerged as winners of the Best Innovative Project of the Year, held in Accra in June 2025.

Led by Philip Cobbinah, our team first dominated the campus round against four competitors before advancing to nationals with our groundbreaking automated robot waiter that captivated judges and attendees alike.
